About Me

I'm a Backend Developer with 5+ years of experience building scalable systems across FinTech, AdTech, Healthcare, and E-commerce. My expertise lies in designing robust APIs, data pipelines, and integrating machine learning models into production environments. With a Master's degree in Bioinformatics from Sharif University and a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering, I bridge computational biology, data science, and software engineering.

My technical portfolio covers automated trading systems, fraud detection pipelines, computer vision applications, and NLP models. I specialize in Python, distributed systems, microservices, and real-time data processing. Notable projects include arbitrage bots for crypto markets, multi-vendor e-commerce platforms, and ML-powered systems for drug-protein interaction prediction and Persian text analysis.

As a researcher, I've published papers on brain tumor detection using fuzzy convolutional neural networks, IoT-based waste management systems, and smart home automation. My work has appeared in IEEE conferences and academic journals, showing my ability to contribute to both practical software and theoretical research in AI and distributed systems.

I'm passionate about the intersection of machine learning and backend engineering, delivering measurable performance and business impact. For more details, see my Projects and Publications pages.
